Privacy Policy

Last updated: 09 March 2026

1. Overview

This extension is designed to operate locally within the user's browser environment. It does not operate external servers and does not provide remote services.

2. Information Collection

The extension does not collect, store, transmit, or sell personal information. No browsing history, personal identifiers, or usage analytics are gathered by the developer.

3. Local Processing

Any data entered by the user is processed locally within the browser session. The extension does not permanently store user input and does not transmit it to external systems controlled by the developer.

4. Third-Party Websites

The extension may interact with third-party websites solely to perform its intended functionality within the browser. No data is shared with the developer or unrelated third parties.

5. Data Security

All processing occurs within the user's browser environment. The developer does not have access to user activity or information.

This Privacy Policy may be updated if the extension’s functionality changes. Updates will be reflected on this page.
